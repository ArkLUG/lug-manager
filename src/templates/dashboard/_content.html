Subscribe to LUG Calendar

All meetings and events sync automatically to your calendar app. Copy the URL below and add it as a subscription — your calendar will stay up to date.

Apple Calendar
  1. Open Calendar app
  2. File → New Calendar Subscription
  3. Paste the URL above
  4. Click Subscribe
Google Calendar
  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Click "+" next to "Other calendars"
  3. Choose "From URL"
  4. Paste the URL above
  5. Click "Add calendar"
Outlook
  1. Open Outlook Calendar
  2. Add calendar → Subscribe from web
  3. Paste the URL above
  4. Click Import
